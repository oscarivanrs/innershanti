EFT is a simple, non-intrusive, method of defusing emotional issues and reducing the level of stress most people feel in everyday life. It is a technique developed in the USA by Gary Craig and is now becoming more widely used and taught. It is not hypnotherapy or psychotherapy. EFT can be used regularly on a self-help basis whenever an emotional issue, fear or phobia occurs in your life. EFT works on balancing our emotional & physical energy, some times lifes adverse effects leave us feeling emotionally and physically drained, EFT works on helping one to release negative energy.

How is EFT carried out?

By tapping on certain meridian points in the body. Eye movement, humming and an affirmation that will help you release negative energy. This treatment is carried out with the client sitting or lying down fully clothed.

How does EFT work?

EFT works by releasing negative energy and blockages.
Simple issues may be cleared with one round of EFT. More complex ones may require repeat rounds with the issue being dealt with from a different perspective each time.
